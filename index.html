About Me

Hey there! I'm Leandro. I was born in Kissimmee, Florida, spent a large chunk of my childhood in Las Vegas, Nevada, before moving back down to Florida and have been living here ever since! In 2017, I graduated from Hialeah Gardens H.S. in Hialeah, FL. After completing some courses in Graphic Design, I decided to change majors and tried a career in trades as an electrician. I did a lot of soul searching before my gut told me to finally go with web development. Now enrolled as a student in the University of Central Florida's Coding Bootcamp as of December 7, 2020, I'm finally pursuing something that is fulfilling and challenges me to grow. I've learned to work with HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, Jquery, APIs, and much more. My goal is to one day become a full stack developer and start my own company. Thanks for visiting my web site.
